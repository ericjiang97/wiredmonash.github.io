Date & Time 7 September, 6:00 - 8:00PM

Have your say on the future direction of the club.

It is that time of year again - the Annual General Meeting. This is where you get to decide on who will be part of the 2016-17 committee, and voice your opinions on where the club should be going. Did we mention there's free pizza?

We strongly urge that ALL members attend the AGM. We also strongly urge that those people who wish to be part of the committee attend the AGM so you can put your name forward.
