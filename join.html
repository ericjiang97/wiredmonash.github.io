Join WIRED in 2015

If you like tech, then we are the club for you.

Who are we?

WIRED Monash is the principal student society for students in the Faculty of Information Technology and those who share a curiosity for all things digital.

We provide an environment where students can meet other like-minded students to collaborate on projects, and share opinions on the latest tech. We also help students expand their knowledge through workshops, talks and providing them access to the latest technology.

In addition, we provide a bridge between students and the IT industry - connecting students with some of the country's leading tech employers and help further their career prospects.

Why join?

Need some reasons to join our club? Here are some...

Events and Competitions

We have many events and competitions this year to cater for everyone - from UNIHACK to CySCA, Trivia Night to Industry Night.

Free BBQs

We hold a couple of BBQs during each semester - and all of them are free for members. Who doesn't love free food?

Internships and Careers

Looking for a job or want to build up your resume? We will notify you of any internships or career opportunities that comes.

Academic Support

If you have any questions about your course or subject, we will try to help and point you in the right direction.

Oculus Rift

We have our own Oculus Rift DK2! WIRED members will be allowed to borrow it to help develop cool things, or just for fun.

What's coming up?

From fun activities to networking opportunities, we have a jam-packed calendar this year. Here are some upcoming events we have planned for the year.

UNIHACK 2015

Do you think you can make the next big thing? Melbourne's premier student hackathon is coming back later this year, and it promises to be bigger and better!

Industry Night

A night where we connect IT students with some of Australia's leading tech employers. A must-attend for those in their final year or second-last year of study.

Trivia Night

Think you know everything there is to know about tech and geek culture? The annual WIRED Trivia Night is coming back later this year.

Movie Nights

Get your friends, grab some popcorn, and relax from the stresses of uni by watching a movie.

Tech Talks

We bring experts and enthusiasts to Monash to discuss the latest interesting and emerging tech trends and issues - like data retention to AI.

First Years Lunch

A free BBQ for the first years! Make new friends, meet the IT staff and say hello to the WIRED Executive.

Sign Up!

Interested in becoming a member of the best IT club on campus? Membership costs $5 per year, or is FREE if you have a MSA card.

In order to become a member of WIRED, simply visit our one of our events and BBQs and we will sign you up on the spot!
