PwC

PwC is one of Australia's leading professional services firms, bringing the power of our global network of firms to help Australian businesses, not-for-profit organisations and governments assess their performance and improve the way they work. Having grown from a one-man Melbourne accountancy practice in 1874 to the worldwide merger of Price Waterhouse and Coopers & Lybrand in 1998, PwC Australia now employs more than 6,000 people.

Monash Employment and Careers Development

Monash University Employment and Career Development provide a range of employment and career services to students, graduates, faculties, departments, and employers. Monash students and graduates have access to fairs, forums, seminars, information sessions, and career development programs run throughout the year which can improve employability and skills as well as provide the opportunity to network and connect with employers. Employment and Career Development also offer appointments with careers consultants and resume and cover letter checking.
