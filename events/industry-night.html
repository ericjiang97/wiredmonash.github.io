Tech Industry Night

Presented by

WIRED, SMEE and MECC

Date & Time 23 March, 6:30 - 9:00PM

Location Engineering Halls EH2-4, 23 College Walk

Price FREE for everyone

Dress Code Smart Casual

WIRED Monash, SMEE and MECC are teaming up to connect IT, Electrical Engineering and Mechatronics students with representatives of some the country's leading tech employers looking for talent.

With graduate program applications already opened and many vacation work applications opening soon, this will be a great chance for students to network with industry representatives, learn about the opportunities available at various companies, and find more about the application processes. First years are also invited to come along, as this is the perfect time to start exploring and familiarising yourselves with these companies.

The event is FREE for everyone to attend, regardless if you are a member or not. Light refreshments will be served at around 7:30PM. The dress code is smart casual (i.e. wear something nice, but nothing too formal).

About Us

WIRED Monash is the student club for those studying IT or those who are curious about everything digital at Monash University Clayton. Our mission is to bring the best of IT to the Monash community.

The Society of Monash Electrical Engineers (SMEE) is a student run social organisation formed to bring together Electrical Engineering Students at Monash to increase networking and awareness.

Mechatronics Engineering Clayton Club (MECC) was formed to popularise and promote awareness of Mechatronics Engineering among students and companies.
