Get involved

Got ideas, need a place to show off your skills, or want to be more active on campus? Join the WIRED Monash team!

Why you should get involved?

Getting involved with WIRED Monash will help you impress your future employers and boost your chances in getting a job.

We will help you develop your leadership, communication, and event organisating skills. In addition, if you are a creative person (like a photographer or graphic designer), we will give you the opportunity to help build up your portfolio and showcase your skills.

You do not need to be an executive member to get involved. We have plenty of opportunities for everyone to be active on campus - such as helping us run our initiatives like CoderDojo, designing and coming up of ways to promote our club to members, or even just volunteering a couple of hours and help us out during our events.
